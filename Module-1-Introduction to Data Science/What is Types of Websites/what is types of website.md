# Types of Websites ?



* ##### ***Static vs. Dynamic: Static sites display information that doesn't change, while dynamic sites allow for user interaction and often require a login.*** 





* ##### ***Web Portals: Provide a gateway to information from various sources, often used for internal purposes within an organization.***

##### 

#####  

* ##### ***Landing Page: A single-page site designed with a specific call to action, such as signing up for a newsletter or purchasing a product.***

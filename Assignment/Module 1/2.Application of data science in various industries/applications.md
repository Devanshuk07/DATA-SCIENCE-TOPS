##### There are various field in which data science has been used such as followed:



* ##### Healthcare
* ##### Finance
* ##### Retail
* ##### E-Commerce
* ##### Share Market
* ##### Politics



##### Here are some examples where data science can be applied in various fields.



### Healthcare



* ##### Gathering of information of people who get healthcare under government scheme.
* ##### Classifying the different government scheme and number of people who get care under the specified gov scheme.
* ##### Distinguish between the people-weather their is male, female , children.



# E-Commerce



* ###### E-commerce websites like flipkart, amazon, snapdeal who deals with the requirement of people in basic life style or household goods.
* ###### Predicting the items on the basic of past prediction that in near future or in near sale this will be required more or will be in demand.
* ###### Now on that prediction gathering or making the full stock of the required items so that the customers should be given the best and proper service.



# Share Market
